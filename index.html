Zainab Al-Eqabi

My life

Born on August 19, 1990 in Baghdad, she completed her studies in the United Arab Emirates, graduated from Sharjah University as a pharmacist with honors and wanted the privilege to say that the disabled person can distinguish and can turn all the negative energy into positive. Using her father's hand and starting to knock with a hammer and it turns out that this hand was a bomb left over from the Second Gulf War, a medical error occurred during the course of treatment due to the injuries of the bomb, resulting in the amputation of her leg. Zainab completed her life with her prosthetic leg (the iron piece and around it a cosmetic part that looks like a natural man), surprised people by removing the cosmetic part of her artificial leg after inviting her to be a speaker at a conference to tell people that's who I am. Zainab's goal is to change the perception of the society about the disabled and prove that the disabled can reach, participate and excel and can be the first

My work

Zainab create the "Disabled and Proud" facebook page, writing her diary and publishing many videos to encourage people with disabilities to continue and challenge, and the most important of which is her writing on her page "My difference is what distinguishes me, and my disability is what makes my life a flavor that many miss." She participated in many Iraqi, Arab and international conferences, most notably TED X Baghdad 2014, to inspire hope for the disabled, as well as to participate in the World Peace Festival held in Baghdad in 2015. In 2015, she participated in a marathon around the Seven Emirates in collaboration with the Galilee Foundation, which lasted for 12 days for a distance of 575 kilometres, and the profit of the charity marathon was for the rehabilitation of children with disabilities. In 2015, she signed a contract with Auto-Book and Limbitich, the largest company in the field of prosthetics, to be the first Arab Ambassador in the world for people with special needs, thus becoming the first Arab to take this position and be the signing of the contract and obtaining the title of ambassador is the realization of one of her biggest dreams.
